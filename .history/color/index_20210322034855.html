BIRD is informational in nature so we wanted to have a selection of grays to reflect that aspect of our app.
We also wanted to create a fun experience for our users so we chose orange and pastelle pink as highlights.
The orange represents fascination, and pink is used to sooth users, as well as pair with the orange.
Our logo colours BIRD Red and Bird Maroon represent aid, as we aim to help educate our users.
